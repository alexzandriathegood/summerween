New Haven • June 24–27

Summerween Festival

Summerween brings spooky summer fun to New Haven through film, creative activities, and community gatherings across the city.

Inspired by the growing love for horror and supernatural storytelling beyond the fall season, Summerween invites residents to celebrate the eerie side of summer through playful events and local partnerships.
