New Haven • June 24–27

Summerween Festival

This summer, a touch of Halloween arrives early. Summerween is a new community-driven festival bringing spooky film, creative activities, and themed gatherings to locations across New Haven.

Inspired by the growing love for horror and supernatural storytelling beyond the fall season, Summerween invites residents to celebrate the eerie side of summer through playful, city-wide events designed to bring people together.

Prospectus

Why Summerween

Summerween responds to a growing cultural interest in horror and supernatural storytelling beyond the traditional Halloween season. By placing these experiences in the middle of summer, the festival creates a unique contrast—warm weather paired with eerie themes—inviting new audiences to engage with the genre in unexpected ways.

The selected programming blends film, community participation, and local collaboration, positioning Summerween as both a cultural event and a neighborhood experience.
