About Me

Alexzandria is a self-taught artist based in Connecticut and California who blends digital and traditional mediums to explore themes of identity, memory, and futurism. Drawing from her ancestral heritage in dollmaking and storytelling, she challenges conventional narratives and reimagines the roles of race, gender, and history in miniature scale.
